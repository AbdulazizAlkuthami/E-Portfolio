Statement of Interdisciplinary Understanding

Before taking the IDS 3933, I thought interdisciplinary studies were about mixing different subjects to achieve a bigger goal. I also believed it was a major for people who wanted to learn about more than one thing and not be limited to a single field of studies.

This course has really opened my eyes. At first, I thought interdisciplinary was just about putting different topics together. But now I understand that there's more to it. There's interdisciplinary, multidisciplinary, and transdisciplinary, each with its own way of combining subjects.

My understanding of this has changed the way I see my future. I'm focused on writing and technology, and I want to use what I've learned in these areas to become good at working with technology, hopefully at a big tech company. I currently have a friend who has told me they've been working on writing a manual for future employees to go by when using a company software, and I think I could combine my two areas of concetration and do the same for the future companies I will work for. I also plan to do this by combining my studies with extra things like certifications and bootcamps.

In summary, this course taught me that interdisciplinary studies are not just about studying different subjects together. It's about how these subjects can work together in a useful way. I plan to use my writing and tech skills together in my future job, and I think this will make me better at what I do.
